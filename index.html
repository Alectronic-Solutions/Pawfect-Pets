FAQ

Do you offer 24/7 emergency services?
Yes, we have a veterinarian on call 24/7 for urgent care. Please call our main line and follow the prompts for emergency service.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, CareCredit, and pet insurance direct billing for select providers.
Do I need an appointment for grooming?
Yes, our grooming salon operates by appointment only to ensure each pet receives dedicated attention. We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance.
What vaccinations are required for training classes?
For the safety of all pets, we require proof of Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella vaccinations prior to attending any group classes.
